Generate a Gemini/AI image prompt that matches the We-Commerce photo style. Read BRAND.md and wecommerce-photo-style-guide.md first.

## Arguments

$ARGUMENTS should describe what the photo needs to show (e.g., "woman using phone at coffee shop", "two people collaborating on laptop", "person walking in city checking phone").

## Base Style (always include)

```
Warm vintage film photography style, Kodak Portra 400 film emulation.
Soft natural window light, shallow depth of field with creamy bokeh.
Muted desaturated colors with warm amber-golden tones. Lifted shadows
with milky faded blacks, no harsh contrast. Subtle film grain texture.
Warm honey skin tones. Earth-toned color palette. Intimate close-up
framing. Candid authentic moment, not posed.
```

## Rules

- Always include the base style prompt
- Wardrobe: cream, beige, olive, rust, muted sage — never bright colors, neon, suits, or formal wear
- Settings: cafe, home office, couch, city street, outdoor leisure — never corporate boardrooms, studios, or gyms
- Lighting: soft natural light, no flash — never studio lighting or harsh shadows
- Mood: candid, authentic, warm, approachable — never posed, editorial, corporate, or formal
- DOF: shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh — never everything in focus
- Max 2-3 people per image, intimate scale
- Phones/devices as central props when relevant
- If the image will have a UI overlay composited on top, frame subject to one side leaving open space

## Output

Print the complete prompt ready to paste into an image generator. Include negative prompt if the model supports it.
