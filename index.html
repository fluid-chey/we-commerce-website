The Problem

The problem isn't one thing. It's everything not connecting.

You know what this feels like. The payment processor doesn't talk to the commission engine. Attribution breaks and the wrong rep gets credit. A sync drops and suddenly commissions are wrong. A promo code doesn't fire because two systems have conflicting data.

Your team spends its week keeping this Frankenstein alive.

Your reps — the people your entire model depends on — quietly stop trusting the system. They share a link, someone else gets credit, and they learn a hard lesson: using the corporate platform is a risk. So they stop.

When reps stop sharing, traffic dies. When traffic dies, growth stalls. The technology was supposed to help. Instead it became the obstacle.

Artificial Intelligence

AI that makes your reps better — not redundant.

The "WE" in We-Commerce is not an accident. Direct selling works because real people share products with other real people they actually know and trust. That relationship is the product. You cannot automate it. You should not try.

Fluid uses AI to amplify that — not replace it. Catchups, the AI-powered assistant built into the rep App, monitors contact activity across your entire field and surfaces the right signal at the right moment.
