Hand-drawn X-mark textures for emphasis and decorative crosses.

## Available Assets

Assets: `199`, `218`

Additional cross/X elements: `Coss_004`, `Coss_007`, `Coss_014`

## Usage Rules

- Use as emphasis marks or decorative accents
- Apply `mix-blend-mode: screen` and low opacity (0.10-0.20)
- Good for marking rejected/old approaches in comparison posts
- Reference via `/api/brand-assets/serve/{name}`
